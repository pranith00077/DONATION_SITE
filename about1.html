About the Trust

Overview

This trust was started in 2005 by Lakshmi. Our mission is to support and uplift the underprivileged members of our community.

Our People

We currently have over 40 members in our trust, including:

  • Children: 10
  • Old People: 20+

Our Kitchen

We have a fully equipped kitchen to prepare nutritious meals for our members.

Education for Children

We are dedicated to making sure that children in our care receive a proper education.

Support for Orphans

We help orphans by providing shelter, food, and education.

Healthcare for the Handicapped

We provide healthy treatment and care for handicapped individuals.

Clothing and Food Distribution

We ensure that all our members receive adequate clothing and food.

Founder

The trust was founded by Lakshmi, who has dedicated her life to helping those in need.
