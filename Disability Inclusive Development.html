Disability Inclusive Development

Empowering People with Disabilities

Our Disability Inclusive Development program aims to create equal opportunities for persons with disabilities (PwDs) through education, skill-building, and community integration. We work to eliminate stigma and ensure full participation in society.

Key Initiatives

  • Early intervention and rehabilitation services
  • Inclusive education support in mainstream schools
  • Vocational training and job placements
  • Awareness and advocacy campaigns

Success Stories

Meet Anitha, a visually impaired student who graduated with honors through our support program. With access to learning tools and mentorship, she now leads workshops for others.

Partner With Us

We welcome partnerships with organizations, corporates, and individuals who want to make a difference. Reach out to us to volunteer, sponsor assistive devices, or support job placement drives.
