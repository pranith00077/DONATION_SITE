Rural Hospitals & Clinics

Bringing Healthcare to the Last Mile

Millions in rural areas still walk miles to see a doctor. Our rural healthcare program brings essential medical services closer to home through clinics, mobile vans, and trained health workers — improving health outcomes, especially for women and children.

Our Healthcare Projects

  • Setting up and supporting village health clinics
  • Running mobile health vans with regular schedules
  • Supporting community hospitals with doctors and equipment
  • Using telemedicine to reach remote villages

Health Services We Offer

  • Basic health check-ups and diagnostics
  • Maternal and child health support
  • Free medicines and vitamin distribution
  • Immunization drives and awareness campaigns

Empowering Rural Health Workers

We train and support ASHA workers, nurses, and community volunteers to become local health champions who can recognize danger signs, promote hygiene, and support basic care.

120+ Villages Reached
240+ Health Camps Conducted
