Ecology & Sustainable Livelihoods

Protecting Nature, Sustaining Lives

Our ecology and sustainable livelihoods program empowers rural communities to conserve natural resources while creating green jobs and income opportunities. We believe that environmental restoration and economic development can go hand-in-hand.

Our Environmental Work

  • Tree plantation drives and forest regeneration
  • Rainwater harvesting and watershed development
  • Soil conservation and organic farming promotion
  • Transition to clean energy: solar and biogas

Green Livelihoods

We train and support people to earn income through sustainable, nature-friendly work:

  • Beekeeping and honey production
  • Bamboo crafts and eco-handicrafts
  • Vermicomposting and organic inputs
  • Eco-tourism and forest guiding

Impact So Far

12,000+ Trees Planted
350+ Families Trained
60+ Villages Covered
400+ Eco-jobs Created
